Al Ahly SC

History of the club

The idea of establishing Al Ahly in the first decade of the century was raised by Omar Lotfy and it was founded in 24 april 1907.

In 1918 the club first official participation was held. After Al Ahly refused to compete with the foreign allies for the first edition of the Sultan Husain Cup in 1917, the club decided to participate in the 1918 tournament.

Al Ahly largest score against his rivarly Zamalek SC was 6-0 in 2001.
